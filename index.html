ABOUT ME

I'm Dandem — a self-taught full-stack developer who learned to code in parallel with a demanding day job. Hundreds of failed projects never stopped me. Every bug was fuel. Every error was a teacher.

Born and raised in rural Romania, now based in Belgium. For years I worked in the physical trades while teaching myself programming alone at night — no courses, no mentor, no shortcuts.

The same hands that built things in the physical world now build digital infrastructure. I believe in learning by doing, in breaking things and rebuilding them better — and in the refusal to accept that where you start defines where you end up.

"The circuit tattoo on my hand isn't decoration — it's a statement. Every line is the transition from manual labor to digital architecture."

// 04 — my journey

THE STORY

THE BEGINNING
Romania → Belgium
Grew up in rural Romania in the 90s-2000s — hard but happy childhood. Moved to Belgium and built a living with my hands. Physical work, long days. But the dream of building something digital never faded.
THE GRIND
Learning to Code After Work
After long days of physical work, I'd come home and teach myself programming. Hundreds of failed attempts. No courses, no mentor, no bootcamp — just raw determination and a screen glowing in the dark. Every error message was a teacher.
THE BUILD
First Real Projects
Built Dandemartai, launched a private game server, experimented with AI integrations. Each project leveled up my skills from basic HTML to full-stack development, database architecture, and server management. Every failure left a lesson.
THE MARK
The Circuit Tattoo
Had an electric circuit tattooed on my hand — not decoration, but a statement. A permanent reminder of the transition from the physical world to digital architecture. The same hands, a completely different build.
NOW — 2026
The Escape is Live
My e-invoicing platform is live on the PEPPOL network. First real invoices sent. First real clients. Building the tool that bridges Belgium's 2026 mandatory e-invoicing transition — the bridge from physical worker to tech entrepreneur.
THE FUTURE
No Limits
The goal isn't just financial freedom — it's proof that anyone, from anywhere, can build their way out through code, determination, and the refusal to quit. This is just the beginning.
