MY JOURNEY

THE BEGINNING
From Romania to Belgium
Grew up in rural Romania. Moved to Belgium and started working in construction — roofing, hard physical labor, rain or shine. But the dream of building something digital never faded.
THE GRIND
Learning to Code After Work
After 8-10 hour construction days, I'd come home and teach myself programming. Hundreds of failed attempts. No courses, no mentor — just raw determination and a screen. Every error message was a teacher.
THE BUILD
First Real Projects
Built Dandemartai, launched a server, experimented with AI integrations. Each project leveled up my skills from basic HTML to full-stack development, database architecture, and server management.
NOW — 2026
Tools & The Escape
Building a PEPPOL-compliant e-invoicing platform for Belgium's mandatory 2026 transition. This is the project that changes everything — the bridge from construction worker to tech entrepreneur.
THE FUTURE
No Limits
The goal isn't just financial freedom — it's proof that anyone, from anywhere, can build their way out through code, determination, and the refusal to quit. This is just the beginning.
